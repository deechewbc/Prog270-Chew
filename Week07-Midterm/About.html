Historical Event

There are many historical things in our lives. Mine is going to a safari and seeing animals that are normally caged but walking or running free. I had the opportunity of seeing a male and female lion.

What is so historical about a male lion? Think about these three facts:
1. The mane
2. Eyes
3. Roar

What other animal in the universe has this mane. This mane in my opinion makes lion The king of the jungle. At first glance I want to run my fingers through the mane because it looks so soft.

Now lets think about the female lion. Is it as fierce looking as the male lion?

Lets think of 3 historical events about the female lion?
1. Hunt together with other female lions
2. Prefer male lions with bigger and darker mane
3. Protect male lions during fights

During my safari we had the opportunity to see male and female lion with their cubs. The cubs were playing with the female lion as the male watched. I got the sense he was busy protecting his family.

Have you ever wondered how lions communicate? I cant imagine the male lion roaring all night and day at the female or the cubs. I asked this question to my safari guide. Surprise surprise they communicate by their EARS. Who would have guessed. So every couple of seconds I would see the male move his ears as well as the female. It is fascinating to watch communication without sound.

Next time in the zoo check out the male lion's ears. How often does it twitch or move.
